# Retail-Sales-Dashboard
![](https://github.com/MarvelousOtis/Retail-Sales-Dashboard/blob/main/dashboard.png)

## Introduction:
This dashboard analyzes a retail sales dataset to uncover insights into customer behavior, sales trends, and product performance.

The goal is to support business decision-making by identifying patterns in customer demographics, purchasing habits, and seasonal sales performance.

---

## Key Metrics:
- What is the total revenue generated from sales?
- How many total orders and customers are recorded?
- What is the Average Order Value (AOV)?
- How does customer age and gender influence purchasing behavior?
- Are there discernible patterns in sales across different time periods?
- Which product categories hold the highest appeal among customers?
- What are the relationships between age, spending, and product preferences?
- How do customers adapt their shopping habits during seasonal trends?
- Are there distinct purchasing behaviors based on basket size?
- What insights can be derived from product price distribution?

---

## Analysis:

**Customer Demographics**
- Customers aged 25–44 contribute the highest share of total revenue.
- Female customers slightly outperform male customers in purchasing activity.

**Sales Trend (Time Analysis)**
- Sales peak in May and October.
- A noticeable decline occurs in September, indicating seasonal variation.

**Product Category Performance**
- Electronics is the top-performing category.
- Clothing and Beauty categories also contribute significantly.

**Age, Spending & Preferences**
- Middle-aged customers (25–44) spend more compared to other groups.
- Younger customers contribute the least, while older customers show moderate spending.

**Seasonal Behavior**
- Customers increase spending during peak months and reduce activity during low periods.
- This suggests seasonal or economic influences on purchasing behavior.

**Basket Size Behavior**
- Most transactions involve small basket sizes.
- Customers tend to purchase fewer items per transaction, indicating low bulk buying.

**Price Distribution Insight**
- Electronics contributes higher revenue, suggesting higher-priced items.
- Clothing and Beauty reflect consistent demand with lower-priced products.

## Conclusion & Recommendations
The dashboard reveals that customer demographics, product category, and seasonal trends significantly influence sales performance.

The most valuable customers are aged 25–44, and purchasing behavior is driven by both time-based trends and product demand.

**Recommendations**
- Target marketing campaigns toward the 25–44 age group
- Increase promotions during low-performing months (e.g., September)
- Focus on high-performing categories like Electronics
- Introduce bundle offers to increase basket size and AOV
- Improve engagement strategies during off-peak periods
